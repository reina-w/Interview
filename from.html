This is Shanghai, China .
Amanda has been living there for years before she moved to the US at the age of 11.

As it is shown on the right, Yu Garden is one of the most popular place in Shanghai and there are all kinds of snacks and beautiful decorations.
Every Lantern Festival, the fairs are held in Yu Garden that people all gather to enjoy the sight of differently shaped lanterns that illuminate the streets.

Huaihai Middle Road is Amanda's favorite place in Shanghai, which used to be the French Concession in the 20th century.
Along both sides of the road, under the shadow of the beautiful sycamores, there are different styles of buildings from the last century, with a spirit of the times.
